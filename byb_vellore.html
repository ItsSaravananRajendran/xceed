Intro

SP ROBOTIC WORKS OFFERS A LEARNING PACKAGE THAT INCLUDES PROGRESSIVELY CHALLENGING EXPERIMENTS BACKED WITH AN ONLINE LEARNING PLATFORM. SP Robotic Works, one of India’s leading edu-tech companies helping students explore robotics through hands on curriculum guided by a unique gamified online platform. We offer programs with various levels of proficiency in which students, from 11+ years to graduates up to 50+ years - learn from the basics of electronics to building a walking robot. Our intent is to inspire Students to take on challenges confidently; relying on the skills they have developed through the robotic course.

  • They gain an understanding of how to use the ergonomic, customizable development stations to create robots from the basic, applying the most complex concepts.
  • They learn how to implement the new functionalities, exploring the up-to-date technologies, on each robot for highly dynamic, autonomous functionality. Working together with team members to seek creative solutions and collaborate on the design and implementation of their solutions to robotic challenges instils the team spirit in them.
  • We help them acquire a thorough understanding of the capabilities of what they like and what their interests are in and develop skills to sharpen their mere interests into their future success

Intro

How Gmaps gives results of place near to your locality, How Ola and other cab apps provide you the nearest cabs available, How short and traffic less paths are found…….It’s not simple data analysis .They are all the real time spatial data. Spatial analysis is a type of geographical analysis which seeks to explain patterns of human behavior and its spatial expression in terms of mathematics and geometry, that is, locational analysis. Do you think Gmaps gives you all the data you require? Absolutely no. Participants will be taught to download their required map data of any area and to analyze them and to create result layers using open source software. Forgot your old school atlases. How are those maps published now a days? Maps a not just points and marker data they help to visualize simple population data to complex temporal data. We train the participants to publish their own spatial data web mapping.
